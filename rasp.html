Raspberry Pi Software-Defined Radio

A real-time SDR implemented using C++ and Python that offers support for mono and stereo FM channels. The project is capable of multithreading and RDS processing. It captures FM signals from your local broadcasters and expands on the knowledge of programming and digital signal processing, resulting in a fully functional radio.
