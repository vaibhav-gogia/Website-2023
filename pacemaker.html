Cardiac Pacemaker

A Simulink-based pacemaker that offers operation in several modes such as AOO, VOO, AAI, etc. and features rate-adaptive modes based on the user's physical activity. The pacemaker comes with an interactive app (DCM: Device-Controller Monitor), visualized and built using Processing to control the pacemaker.
